Our Mission

We exist to provide a place of worship based on the beliefs of the spiritualist church. We want to build a sense of unity and fellowship within the spiritualist and wider community by creating a welcoming and inclusive environment where people feel loved, accepted and supported.

We will provide opportunities for individuals to use the gifts and talents in service to others.

We will foster deeper relationships and understanding of principles, encouraging personal transformations.
